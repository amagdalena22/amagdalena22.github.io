Ballet Variations

Variations are ballet solo pieces that dancers often learn to improve their technique and skills. Variations can be presented at recitals or competitions such as the Youth American Grand Prix, and often variations appear in larger productions such as the Nutcracker.

Variations can also be called pas seuls because pas seul means to dance alone.

I researched the origins of ballet and a few variations that people at my ballet studio are working on this spring. Talisman is the one I'm performing!
