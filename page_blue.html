Storyline Costumes

This variation is part of the ballet Sleeping Beauty choreographed by Marius Petipa and with music composed by Pyotr Tchaikovsky. The ballet begins at Princess Aurora's christening, where fairies such as the Lilac Fairy come to give her gifts. The evil fairy, Carrabosse, arrives, furious that she didn't receive an invitation to the christening, and curses Aurora that when she turns 16 she will prick her finger on a spindle and die. All the Lilac Fairy has the power to do is to alter the curse so that Aurora will not die but will fall into a 100-year sleep that will be ended by the kiss of a handsome prince.
Aurora's sixteenth birthday comes, and she pricks her finger on a spindle, as predicted. The Lilac Fairy casts a spell of slumber for 100 years on the rest of the kingdom so they will awaken when Aurora does. After 100 years, Prince Desire meets with the Lilac Fairy in the forest, who tells him to go wake up Princess Aurora and break the spell. He kisses her, the kingdom wakes up, and Aurora and Prince Desire get married.
Characters come from far and wide to attend the wedding, including Puss and Boots and Bluebird, and this variation occurs when Bluebird comes to celebrate the wedding.

  • This costume is bright blue and sometimes bird-like with feathers.
  • Bluebird usually has a tutu, but is often slightly longer than a classic tutu.
  • There is a beautifully intricate headpiece associated with this costume made with blue and gold or silver jewels.
