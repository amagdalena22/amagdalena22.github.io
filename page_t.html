Talisman Variation

What would you like to learn about this variation?

Storyline Costumes

Talisman is from a ballet called The Talisman choreographed by Marius Petipa and composed by Riccardo Drigo. In the ballet, the queen of the heavens Amraviti sends her daughter Ella down to Earth to prove she can resist mortal love. Ella is given a talisman, and if Ella fails the test by losing the talisman, she will lose her immortality and will remain with the mortals on Earth.
On Earth, Ella meets Noureddin, who falls in love with her, and when he learns the truth of the talisman, he steals it from her. Ella tries to get the talisman back but falls in love with Noureddin and chooses to forfeit immortality and stay on Earth with him.
This variation is part of the pas d'action between Noureddin and his fiance in Act II of the ballet.

  • The time period of this ballet meant a tutu wasn't worn but instead a dress.
  • The dress for the variation is usually lightly colored with white, blue, or peach.
  • This variation is usually done en pointe.
  • The costume can have a gold cash across the bodice.
