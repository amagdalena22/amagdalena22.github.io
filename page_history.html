Origins of Ballet

Ballet originated in Italy in the Renaissance during the 1400s, and Catherine de Medici's marraige into French royalty spread ballet to France for the first time. Louis XIV, the Sun King, loved dance, and he popularized ballet in aristocratic courts. At the time, audience members would join in and participate in the performances at the end of the ballet.
